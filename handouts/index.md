# Systematic Theology Handouts

These handouts are designed for group study, personal reference, and printed use. Each one explores a theological topic through careful exegesis — reading *out of* the text rather than reading modern assumptions back into it.

---

- [Everything the Bible Says About... The Bible (Condensed)](#handouts/the-bible-about-the-bible-condensed.md) — Condensed reference format
- [Everything the Bible Says About... The Bible (Printable)](#handouts/the-bible-about-the-bible-printable.md) — Short printable reference guide
- [Living Word? Closed Textbook? Guide to Life?](#handouts/living-word-closed-textbook.md) — What is the Bible anyway? A comparison of frameworks

## God

- [God and Time: Does the Future Exist?](#handouts/god-and-time.md) — Divine knowledge, the reality of the future, and what it means when Scripture says God "changed His mind"
- [The Wrath of God: Atrocity or Redemption?](#handouts/wrath-and-mercy.md) — Categorizing the "atrocities" of the OT and the categorical errors we commit reading them through a modern lens

## Jesus

- [Jesus: What Does It Mean to Be the Son of God?](#handouts/son-of-god.md) — How different traditions understand Jesus's identity and what sets it apart from all believers being called "sons of God"
