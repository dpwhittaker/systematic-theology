References: 10s trims of VibeVoice voice wavs, auto-transcribed by whisper-large-v3 (built into Dia2). Separate prefix_speaker_1 + prefix_speaker_2 args — much cleaner than Dia1's concat-and-prepend hack.

Key upgrade: Dia2 uses the Mimi codec (Kyutai) instead of DAC, and is a streaming architecture — quality and cadence should be noticeably improved over Dia-1.6B.
