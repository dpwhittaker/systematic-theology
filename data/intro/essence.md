[Intro](#intro/intro 'Parent')
---
Greek philosophy seeks *precise boundaries* and *essential being*.
What IS a thing? What is its *essence*?
Hebrew thought asks: What does it DO? How does it *relate*?

What IS God, apart from what He does?
Greek: *Being* precedes action. Definition captures essence.
Hebrew: God's *character* is known through His [deeds](#intro/events 'Hebrew').
