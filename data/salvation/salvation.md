How are we *made right* with God?

Key questions:
- *Grace* - Unearned favor or enabling power?
- *Faith* - Trust or intellectual agreement?
- *Justification* - Declared righteous or made righteous?
- *Sanctification* - Growing in holiness.
- *Perseverance* - Can you lose salvation?

Related topics: [Atonement](#salvation/atonement 'Drill'), [Covenant](#salvation/covenant 'Hebrew'), [Blood](#salvation/blood 'Hebrew')

The Hebrew approach: Delivered from slavery, adopted into family.
The Greek approach: Forensic declaration, legal standing.
